Hi and welcome to my portfolio

I’m Andreas, an experienced sound engineer working with all sorts of sound production. I graduated at the University of Salford in 2018, and left England shortly thereafter to start my own company.

things i do
services

sound mix

I offer full-range post production services: from pre-production planning, sound editing, and mixdown to whatever format and platform you desire. Anything from regular stereo mixes to complex surround soundscapes to television, film festivals and streaming sites.

sound mix

recording

I offer a variety of recording services. Film/TV-set recordist (boom), dialogue re-recording sessions, foley, custom atmosphere tracks, podcast and radio-shows, multi-channel band & orchestral recordings to name a few.

recording

sound technician

My professional ventures sometimes lead me to a stage. I work with a whole range of analogue as well as digital mixing desks. This includes all kinds of concerts, small pub gigs, theatre, opera, festivals and fine-tuned classical music.

sound technician
